My Work.

Some of my favourtie pieces of work so far...

Cerner Millennium

Integration

Extensive knowledge integrating and developing inbound and outbound interfaces for Cerner Millennium EPR. Additionally, using various tools to troubleshoot integration issues for production and test envrioments. Utilised Cerner tools such as OpenView, SI_Manager, and Discern Visual Developer (IDE)

DrDoctor

Integration

Integrated DrDoctor scheduling with Cerner Millennium EPR to allow automatic notification and reminders sent to patients while reducing the DNA rate of various clinics. Since implementation over 323,589 letters have been uploaded, with 68,317 patients choosing digital letters as their preferred way of receiving letters. Utilised HL7v2.4, JavaScript, SQL, TCP/IP and CCL

Covid-19 Vaccination

Development

An appointment and booking system to schedule Covid-19 vaccinations and boosters for West Suffolk NHS Foundation Trusts vaccination programme. I specifically focused on generating automatic communications to patients such as reminders, digital vaccine cards, and missed appointments. I also dealt with automatic validation of demographic detail to speed up the vaccination process. The Covid-19 Vaccination Team were rewarded with the Lord Lieutenant of Suffolk award for this work. Utilised XML, XSLT, Apache Freemarker, JavaScript, SQL

Personal Portfolio

NHS Opt Out

Integration & Development

Worked on West Suffolk NHS Foundation Trust implementation of National data opt-out enabling patients to opt out from the use of their data for research or planning purposes. Designed and developed the backend logic to process and record the opt-out choice for over 700k+ NHS Numbers on a regular shcedule along with checking new NHS Numbers in real time when they are recorded in the EPR. Utilised SQL, I/O, SSIS and NHS MESH

Interface Dashboard

Development

Designed and developed a dashboard to display statuses of various interface watchlists to promote greater visbility of the interfaces as the Trust solutions became more interconnected. Utilised ASP.NET MVC, C#, WebAPI, Ajax and JavaScript to build this dashboard solution.

OptiMine

Integration & Development

Working in conjunction with University East Anglia, with the use of technology we supported the OptiMine study to detect at risk patients from the hospital EPR, and generate notifications to contact those patients to provide visibility of behaviour change apps. We then tracked interaction with the notifications to then decide whether to send further notfications as a gentle reminder. Utlised SQL, Azure, JavaScript and the Bitly API.
You can read about the study here: UEA Optimine

Medilogik EMS

Integration

Supported the transition to a digital based Endoscopy solution implementing a Demographic and Result based interfaces. Migrated over 80,000 legacy Endoscopy reports into Cerner Millennium EPR. Utilised HL7, JavaScript, RESTful API, SQL, TCP/IP
